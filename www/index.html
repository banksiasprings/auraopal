About & data sources

AuraOpal is an offline-first field map for the Opalton boulder-opal field, Central-West Queensland, anchored on Steven's mining lease (Aug 2025 field trip). It plots his ground-truth finds against the geology, geophysics and mining-tenement layers that drive boulder-opal favourability. It shows the raw data honestly — it does not predict where opal is. Favourability scoring is deliberately deferred until the data layers are complete and calibrated against the ground truth.

⚠ Fossicking / mining legality shown is advisory only, derived from public tenement data. Always verify tenure and permissions with the Queensland Government before entering or digging on any ground. Aboriginal cultural heritage applies everywhere in Queensland and is not shown here — check the Cultural Heritage Duty of Care Guidelines separately.
